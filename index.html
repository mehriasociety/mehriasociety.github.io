Who We Are?

Mehria Medical Society Pakistan is Non Government, Non Political Volunteer Society and our Motto is to Seek pleasure of Almighty Allah by Saving Human lives via facilitating blood transfusion. Through our mobile app, we will provide blood donors across Pakistan with few tabs on finger tips. We will try our level best to meet 100% blood requirements voluntarily throughout Pakistan. For this purpose, we will conduct different seminars and motivational sessions in colleges and universities. We will create awareness among youth of the country.
